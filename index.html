Subtle lessons learnt

Do not be too ashamed or too proud to do what you must, lawful and godly, to achieve your dreams. Do not run your life with another man's time. There's not enough money for me to pursue what I want, but I'm not limited to do what I don't want in order to get what I want. We are afflicted but not constrained. Do not allow yourself to be pressured into ungodliness in any form. There is always a way out.

No matter how short you are, if you go out you will see the sky ☺️.

Our parents and relatives may be limited to assist you, but with time and energy on your side you will succeed if you put them to good use.

Do not be too ashamed or too proud to beg. It is better they tell you they don't have than for you to assume they don't. It is better they refuse than for you not to try out of fear that they would refuse.

But never you ever feel entitled to anything from anyone. This mentality is killing us as a people. Even when you are, don't feel so.

Do not worry about anything at all. So long as you're willing and obedient you shall eat the fruit of the earth.
